Play. Watch. Learn.

One of my favorite things to do in my free time is play video games. The first games that I fell in love with were The Legend of Zelda: Ocrina of Time and Pokemon: Blue version. I have played all subsequent versions of both of those games since then. Four years ago, my boyfriend introduced me to The World of Warcraft. I had never played an MMO game before and loved it. I enjoyed playing mainly DPS Caster type classes. Shortly after that I began watching professional matches of League of Legends. The more I watched the more I enjoyed it and wanted to learn to play the game myself. Last summer I finally downloaded and started playing League. My summoner name is CygnusDeneb9 after the constellation Cygnus, its brightest star Deneb, and its 9 visible stars. The position that I currently in League is Mid Lane but I also like the AD Carry role and the Support role. My favorite professional League of Leagues team is Team SoloMid. I love to watch their weekly games and hope to some day go see a match in person. By watching the pros I learn how better to play the game. I am always looking for new games to play and some day hope to work for a gaming company as a concept artist.
